Floral and decor budgets typically range from 8 to 15 percent of the total wedding budget. Common floral elements include the bridal bouquet, bridesmaids bouquets, boutonnieres, ceremony arch arrangements, and centerpieces. Centerpiece costs range from $75 to $300 each depending on flower types and complexity. Seasonal flowers are significantly cheaper than out-of-season imports. Popular budget-friendly choices include sunflowers, carnations, and greenery. Luxury flowers such as peonies, garden roses, and orchids increase costs substantially. Couples should confirm flower availability and seasonal pricing with their florist at least 6 months before the wedding. Decor items such as candles, linens, chair covers, and lighting are often rented separately from a decor company or the venue.
