Wedding photography packages typically range from $1,500 to $5,000 depending on hours of coverage, number of photographers, and deliverables such as albums or prints. Most couples book a photographer 10 to 14 months before the wedding date. A second shooter is recommended for guest counts above 100 to ensure full coverage of the ceremony and reception simultaneously. Videography is a separate service and averages $2,000 to $4,000 for a highlight reel plus full-length edit. Couples should schedule an engagement shoot to test chemistry with the photographer before the wedding day. Lighting conditions vary significantly between indoor and outdoor venues, so photographers may require additional equipment for dark ballrooms or bright midday outdoor settings. Contracts should specify image delivery timelines, typically 6 to 12 weeks after the event.
