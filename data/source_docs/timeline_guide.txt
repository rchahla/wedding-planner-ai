A standard wedding day timeline begins with hair and makeup 4 to 5 hours before the ceremony. First look photos are typically scheduled 2 hours before the ceremony if desired. Ceremonies average 20 to 45 minutes depending on religious or civil requirements. Cocktail hour follows the ceremony while the couple and wedding party complete portraits. Reception typically begins with introductions, followed by dinner, toasts, first dance, parent dances, cake cutting, and open dancing. Total reception length is usually 4 to 5 hours. Vendors such as caterers, photographers, and DJs have contracted end times and overtime charges apply if the event runs long. A detailed minute-by-minute timeline should be distributed to all vendors at least one week before the wedding date. Buffer time of 15 to 30 minutes should be built in between major events to account for delays.
