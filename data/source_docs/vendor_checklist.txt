Core vendors to book for a wedding include: venue, caterer, photographer, officiant, florist, and DJ or band. Optional vendors include: videographer, hair and makeup artist, cake designer, transportation, and photo booth rental. All vendor contracts should specify the date, hours, services included, payment schedule, and cancellation policy. A deposit of 25 to 50 percent is typically required to secure a booking. Final payments are usually due one to two weeks before the event. Couples should confirm all vendor bookings in writing and follow up with a reminder call two weeks before the wedding. It is advisable to have a point of contact on the wedding day, such as a day-of coordinator, who communicates with all vendors so the couple does not have to manage logistics themselves.
