Music and entertainment are key factors in guest experience. DJ services for a 5-hour reception typically cost $800 to $2,000. Live bands cost significantly more, ranging from $3,000 to $10,000 depending on size and reputation. A DJ is generally more flexible with song requests and can serve as MC. Bands provide a more energetic atmosphere but require a larger stage area and sound system. Ceremonies typically require a separate sound system or acoustic musicians such as a string quartet or solo guitarist. Music volume and curfew restrictions vary by venue and municipality. Couples should provide a must-play list and a do-not-play list to the performer in advance. For outdoor events, amplification permits may be required depending on local bylaws.
